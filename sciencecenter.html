Science Center Startup Game

ABOUT THE PROJECT

This project was created during my internship at the University City Science Center. I created custom playing cards for Scientists, Funders, and Science Center staff members. The game is now in production and is played at the Science Center during tours, giving the public hands-on experience of the Science Center's mission.
