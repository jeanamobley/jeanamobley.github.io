ABOUT THE PROJECT

While working as a graphic design intern at branding agency At Media, we developed an ad campaign for the Delaware River Waterfront Corporation (DRWC) encouraging Philadelphia residents to "rediscover the waterfront." The waterfront attractions include Spruce Street Harbor Park, the Blue Cross RiverRink, and Penn's Landing.
