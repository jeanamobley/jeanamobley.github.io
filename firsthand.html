FirstHand Print Collateral

ABOUT THE PROJECT

While working with At Media, an advertising agency in Philadelphia, I created print materials for FirstHand, a company who creates firsthand STEAM experience for in-need and local youth at the University City Science Center. The materials are meant to be given to possible youth mentors, board members and staff. Along with the print materials, I created custom graphics for thinking putty, a creative toy for children to use while visiting FirstHand.
